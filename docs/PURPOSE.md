## Primary Purpose

**A thought leadership platform answering: "Why should I create my next startup in Uruguay?"**

The site provides depth and authority on the startup ecosystem, financing, legal frameworks, infrastructure, and operational advantages of launching a tech venture in Uruguay, with guidance for founders at all stages.

---

## Core Positioning

### Who This Is For
- **Founders & CxOs** considering market entry or headquarters location
- **Early-stage startups** seeking funding and operational guidance
- **Tech entrepreneurs** evaluating ecosystem maturity
- **Regional players** exploring Uruguay as LATAM/Mercosur gateway

### What They Learn
1. **Ecosystem landscape** — ANDE, ANNI, accelerators, investor networks
2. **Financing mechanisms** — Seed funding, angel investors, venture capital pathways
3. **Legal advantages** — SAS corporate structure, Zonas Francas, Ley de Startups
4. **Infrastructure & talent** — Connectivity, datacenters, tech education
5. **Operations & strategy** — Soft landing, go-to-market, regional positioning
